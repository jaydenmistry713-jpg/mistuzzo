Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal data when you use mistuzzo.com.

Last updated: 5 July 2026

1. Who We Are

Mistuzzo ("we", "us") is a web design business operating in the United Kingdom. We are the data controller for personal data collected through this website. You can contact us about anything in this policy at mistuzzo.marketing@outlook.com or on WhatsApp at +44 7359 713107.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Contact form data: when you request a free demo or download our guide, we collect the details you enter — typically your name, WhatsApp number, business name and social media handle.
  • Cookies and tracking data: if (and only if) you accept cookies on our consent banner, the Meta Pixel collects information about your visit — such as the pages you view and actions you take — using cookies set by Meta.
  • Technical data: our hosting provider (Netlify) may process standard server logs, including IP addresses, for security and operational purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your enquiry and build your free demo website (legal basis: taking steps at your request before entering a contract).
  • To send you the free guide you requested (legal basis: your consent).
  • To measure and improve the performance of our advertising via Meta Pixel (legal basis: your consent, given via the cookie banner).

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not send marketing emails without your consent.

4. Cookies & Meta Pixel

On your first visit you'll see a cookie banner. Nothing that tracks you loads until you click Accept. If you accept, we store your choice in a cookie (mm_cookie_consent) and load the Meta Pixel, which helps us understand how visitors from our adverts use the site. If you decline, no tracking loads and we simply remember your choice so we don't ask again. You can clear your browser cookies at any time to reset this choice. For details on how Meta processes this data, see Meta's Privacy Policy.

5. Who We Share Data With

  • Netlify — hosts this website and processes form submissions on our behalf.
  • Meta Platforms — receives tracking events only if you accept cookies.
  • Elfsight / Google — our reviews widget displays public Google reviews and may set its own functional cookies.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We keep enquiry details for as long as needed to handle your request and any follow-up conversation, after which they are deleted. If you become a client, we keep your details for the duration of our work together and as required for legal and accounting purposes.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data, and the right to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, email mistuzzo.marketing@outlook.com. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the date of the last update shown at the top.
