Who We Are

We're a digital marketing agency on a mission to help small businesses succeed online with affordable, professional services.

Our Story

Mistuzzo Marketing was founded with a simple belief: every small business deserves a professional online presence, regardless of their budget.

We've seen too many talented business owners—caterers, detailers, salons, contractors—doing incredible work but struggling to attract customers because their online presence doesn't reflect the quality of their services. That's where we come in.

We specialize in helping service-based businesses get online quickly and affordably. No complicated jargon, no hidden fees, no month-long timelines. Just professional websites and effective marketing that actually brings in customers.
